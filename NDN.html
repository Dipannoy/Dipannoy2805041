Hybrid Cache Replacement Policy

As an undergraduate researcher, I focused on Content-Centric Networking (CCN), specifically addressing the memory constraints of NDN nodes. Because these nodes have finite content stores, efficient eviction policies are critical for network throughput.

Proposed Algorithm: The Hybrid Policy

My proposed "Hybrid Policy" optimizes the selection of content for eviction by balancing frequency and recency. During simulation, this approach demonstrated superior hit rates compared to standard industrial baselines like LRU (Least Recently Used) and Priority FIFO.

Technical Scope

The research involved simulating network traffic and cache hits using the ndnSIM simulator. The findings contributed to a deeper understanding of how data naming conventions influence caching efficiency in next-generation internet architectures.
